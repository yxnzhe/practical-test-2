My HomeTown




Best Place to Visit in Kuantan, Pahang



Glasshouse Starbucks @ Kuantan

This glasshouse Starbucks is located at Teluk Cempedak beach, the two-storey outlet is only a few metres away from the beach. Every customer gets the opportunity to enjoy their coffee while overlooking the sea.
Address: Jalan Tengku Abu Bakar, No 1 & 2, Pusat Rekreasi, Teluk Cempedak, Kuantan, 25050, Pahang.

Kula Cakes by Lila Wadi

There always seems to be a line outside this small cake shop for the mango cheesecake, which is lovely but a rather brief reward if the queue has been overwhelming. So double up on your order, or go for the pavlova or chocolate cake, too.
Address: 96, Jalan Besar, 25000 Kuantan, Pahang




Bukit Gambang Water Park

Bukit Gambang waterpark have a wave pool with 8 different wave modes, an aqua play structure for children to the longest family raft ride in Malaysia; this park has something for everyone! We are the first park in Malaysia to fully implement a cashless transaction system.
Address: Bukit Gambang Resort City, 26300 Gambang, Kuantan, Pahang, Malaysia.
