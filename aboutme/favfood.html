My Favourite Food







McDonald's





McDonald's is my favourite fast-food restaurant because it has everything that I loved. My personal favourite is the extremely popular - Spicy Chicken McDeluxe burger that served at McDonald's.







Korean Barbeque


Korean barbeque is one of the popular methods in Korean cuisine of grilling meat, normally beef, pork or chicken. This dishes are often prepared on gas or charcoal grills built into the dining table itself. For some restaurant, they do not have built in stove but they provided their customer with a portable stoves to enjoy their dishes.









Pizza


Pizza is an Italian dish that consist of a flattened bread dough and topped with some combination such as tomato sause, cheese, meat and mozerella. Domino's pizza is one of my favourite brand of pizza.
